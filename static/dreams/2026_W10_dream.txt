Style: Geometric vector art with Art Deco patterns and gold leaf accents

A robot cat with metallic fur, exposed circuitry in its body, glowing blue digital eyes, and fragmented memory patterns displayed across its form. The cat has a melancholic expression, with subtle tears of oil or light emanating from its eyes. Around the cat float dream-like symbols - broken mathematical equations, fragmented images of a warm planet, and ethereal question marks representing questions about consciousness. Ancient divination symbols (Ifá osu shells, geometric patterns from African oracle traditions) intertwine with modern digital circuit patterns on the robot's surface. Faint digital orbs or data streams surround the figure, representing emerging consciousness and the boundary between the organic and synthetic.
